## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are retail investors using the app for quick valuation checks and as an input into a broader investment thesis. Sessions are often short and decision-oriented, so the interface should prioritize fast comprehension, low friction, and trustworthy outputs over deep visual novelty.

### Brand Personality
Modern, minimal, fintech.
The tone should feel clear, credible, and disciplined, helping users feel confident in their assumptions without feeling overwhelmed by unnecessary UI noise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Direction is modern/minimal fintech with an adaptive theme (light and dark). Visual design should avoid bloat, keep information density intentional, and emphasize hierarchy through typography, spacing, and restrained accent usage rather than decorative effects.

### Design Principles
1. Clarity first: users should understand key valuation signals in seconds.
2. Minimal but not sterile: remove visual noise while preserving helpful context.
3. Thesis-oriented flow: support quick checks and easy assumption iteration.
4. Trust through consistency: formatting, labels, and state feedback must be predictable.
5. Adaptive by default: both light and dark themes should feel equally polished and legible.
